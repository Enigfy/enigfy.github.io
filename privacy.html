Privacy

Enigfy

Privacy is a first-class concern at Enigfy. Our apps are designed to work without accounts, without tracking, and without third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. Whatever data is needed to run a feature stays on your device wherever possible, and anything that does reach a server is kept to the minimum required for that feature to work.

Each app has its own privacy policy describing exactly what it stores, what (if anything) it transmits, and how long any data is kept:

  • Privacy Policy — Monaco
  • Privacy Policy — Monte Carlo
