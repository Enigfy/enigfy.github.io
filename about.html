About

Enigfy · 2026

Enigfy is an independent iOS game studio. We build small, focused games that are easy to learn and hard to put down.

Our first game, Marienbad, is a strategy game based on the mathematical game of Nim — designed to be beautiful, fair, and endlessly replayable.

More games are in preparation.
