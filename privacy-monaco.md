# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 21, 2026 · Enigfy

## 1. Introduction

Monaco ("the App") is a strategy game developed and published by Enigfy ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

We take your privacy seriously. Monaco is designed to work entirely on your device — it does not collect personal data, does not require an account, and does not transmit any information to external servers.

This policy is accessible within the App (via the Help screen) and at [www.enigfy.com](https://www.enigfy.com), as required by Apple's App Store Review Guidelines (Guideline 5.1).

## 3. What Data the App Collects

Monaco does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Enigfy is not a data controller or data processor under any applicable privacy law because no personal data ever leaves your device or reaches our systems.

### 3.1 Data stored locally on your device

The following information is saved on your device only, using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage. It is never transmitted to Enigfy or any third party:

- Game preferences (sound on/off, preferred starter player, display settings)
- Game history (win/loss records and move counts for up to 100 recent games)
- Pro upgrade status (whether you have purchased the Pro unlock)
- Tutorial and guided-game completion flags
- Advanced mode trial counter (a game count used to determine free trial eligibility for 2-Star and 3-Star modes)

This data is processed only on your device. It is automatically included in your iOS device backups (iCloud or local) under Apple's standard backup policies, which you control in your iOS Settings. Because all data stays on your device, there is no "collection" of data by Enigfy within the meaning of applicable privacy laws.

### 3.2 Data we do not collect

Monaco does not collect or process any of the following:

- Your name, email address, or any contact information
- Your location
- Device identifiers or advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- Usage analytics or crash reports — no analytics SDK is included in the App
- Health data, contacts, photos, camera, or microphone access
- Any information from other apps or websites
- Any data used for tracking you across apps or websites owned by other companies

## 4. How We Use Data

Because Monaco collects no personal data, there is no data use to describe beyond the on-device functionality listed in section 3.1 above. The locally stored preferences and game history are used solely to operate the App on your device — for example, to remember your settings between sessions and to display your game history. No data is used for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the App's core functionality.

## 5. In-App Purchases (Pro Upgrade)

Monaco offers a one-time optional Pro upgrade via Apple's StoreKit framework. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. Enigfy does not receive, process, or store any payment card details or billing information.

When you complete a purchase or restore a previous one, Apple passes a verified transaction receipt to the App. The App reads this receipt solely to confirm whether the Pro unlock is active, and stores only a simple "unlocked: yes/no" flag on your device. No transaction identifiers or Apple ID information are retained by the App.

For information on how Apple handles payment data, please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## 6. Third-Party Services and SDKs

Monaco does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social networking SDKs. No personal data is shared with or transmitted to any third party by the App.

The only third-party framework used is Apple's StoreKit, which is provided by Apple as part of iOS and governed by Apple's own privacy policy. Apple, as the provider of StoreKit, applies data protection standards equivalent to or exceeding those described in this policy, as required by Apple's App Store Review Guidelines.

Monaco does not use any AI or machine-learning services, and no user data is shared with any third-party AI system.

## 7. Data Collected by the App Store

When you download, install, or make a purchase within Monaco, Apple may collect certain information as part of operating the App Store — for example, purchase history associated with your Apple ID, or crash logs submitted via Apple's opt-in diagnostics programme. This data is collected and controlled by Apple, not by Enigfy. Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for details.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Monaco does not knowingly collect any personal information from any user, including children. Because the App collects no personal data whatsoever, it is suitable for all ages.

The applicable minimum age for digital consent varies by jurisdiction — for example, 13 under the United States' COPPA, and 13–16 under the European Union's GDPR (depending on the member state). Regardless of age, no personal data is collected by this App, so no age-based consent is required. Parental controls and family-sharing settings are managed through Apple's Screen Time and Family Sharing features in iOS Settings.

## 10. Data Retention and Deletion

All data stored by the App lives exclusively on your device and is entirely under your control. There is no server-side data and therefore no server-side retention period.

**On-device data is retained indefinitely until you delete it.** You can remove all App data at any time by:

- **Deleting the App** from your device (iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Monaco → Delete App). This immediately and permanently removes all locally stored preferences and game history.
- **Resetting individual preferences** within the App's Settings screen.

Because Enigfy does not hold any data on its servers, there is nothing for us to delete upon request. Deletion is entirely self-service via the methods above.

## 11. Consent and Your Privacy Rights

Because Monaco does not collect personal data, no consent to data collection is sought or required. There is accordingly no consent to revoke.

Standard data-subject rights — including the right of access, rectification, portability, and erasure — are satisfied automatically: the only data that exists is stored locally on your device and you control it entirely via the deletion methods in section 10.

If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, please contact us at [info@enigfy.com](mailto:info@enigfy.com). We will respond within 30 days.

## 12. International Users

Monaco is available worldwide through the Apple App Store. Because no personal data is transmitted to Enigfy or stored on Enigfy's servers, there is no cross-border transfer of personal data.

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom are covered by this policy. As Enigfy does not process personal data, Enigfy does not act as a data controller or data processor within the meaning of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the UK GDPR. No legal basis for processing is required because no processing of personal data occurs.

Users in Switzerland are similarly covered. As no personal data is processed by Enigfy, the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (revDSG, in force since September 2023) does not impose obligations on Enigfy in respect of the App.

Users in California and other US jurisdictions with state privacy laws (including the California Consumer Privacy Act, CCPA) are equally covered. As Monaco does not collect, sell, share, or disclose personal information, the rights afforded under those laws — such as the right to know, delete, and opt out of sale — are satisfied by the App's design.
